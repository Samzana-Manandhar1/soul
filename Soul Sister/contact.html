Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for all unopened products. Opened products can be returned within 14 days if you're not satisfied.
Do you offer free shipping?
Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50. Express shipping options are also available.
Are your products authentic?
Absolutely! All our products are 100% authentic and sourced directly from authorized distributors and brand partners.
Do you test on animals?
We are committed to cruelty free beauty. All our partner brands share our values and do not test on animals.
